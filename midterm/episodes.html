Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

Episode 1

Tanjiro Kamado lives a simple, happy life in the mountains until the day he finds his family slaughtered; Tanjiro's sister, Nezuko, is the sole survivor.

Episode 2

Tanjiro heads for Mt Sagiri with his sister Nezuko, who is now a demon. During the night, Tanjiro smells blood coming from inside a temple and goes to investigate.

Episode 3

Tanjiro encounters fellow Final Selection survivor Zenitsu on his next journey, but has a difficult time with his passive attitude.

Episode 4

The constantly transforming house separates Tanjiro and Zenitsu; Tanjiro encounters a demon musician and a man wearing the head of a boar.

Episode 5

The demon that appeared before Tanjiro and Rengoku after they defeated Enmu was the Upper Three, Akaza.

Episode 6

Akaza visits Muzan Kibutsuji. Guided by a crow, Tanjiro Kamado heads off to the Rengoku residence to convey Kyojuro Rengoku's last messages to his father and little brother.

Episode 7

When day breaks, Tanjiro and the other survivors are welcomed into the Demon Slayer Corps and issued uniforms and Kasugai Crows as messengers.

Episode 8

Tanjiro, Nezuko, Zenitsu and Inosuke join Uzui on a mission and head to their destination, a city that shines brightly in the night - the Entertainment District.
