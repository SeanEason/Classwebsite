Cheesiest Grilled Cheese

Serving 1

This is a quick and simple way on how you can have the cheesiest grilled cheese EVER

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread (recommended)
  • 2 slices of Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 slices of Monterary Jack Cheese
  • 1 slice of Provolone Cheese
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Heat skillet over medium-low flame
  2. Add butter to coat pan
  3. While the butter melts assemble your grilled cheese by placing the cheese in between the two slices of bread
  4. Once butter is completely melted place the sandwhich on to the skillet
  5. Press the sandwich with a spatula to get it it nice and flat
  6. After about 3 minutes check to see if the other side of the sandwich is golden brown
  7. Add more butter to the pan then flip
  8. After another 3 minutes remove the grilled cheese from the skillet and turn off the flame
  9. Place on a plate and ENJOY!
Calories
White Bread 70 per slice
Sharp Cheddar 110 Cals
Monterary Jack 120 Cals
Provolone 210 Cals
Butter 110 Cals per tablespoon
TOTAL 720 Cals
